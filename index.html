About Us

What do we do?

We deliver top-notch service with a personal touch related to eyecare. We've got a soft spot for kids and their eye care needs, handling them with professional expertise and genuine care. With not one, but two full-time optometrists on-site, we've got the expertise to handle any eye-related concern you may have. Our goal?
To improve your overall well-being by optimising your vision. Whether it's work, study, sports, or enjoying your favorite hobbies, we're here to help you unleash your full potential.

Come in and let's make your vision shine!

Our Services

  • Eye Examinations (may be bulk-billed)
  • Spectacles (may be bulk-billed to private Health Fund optical cover)
  • Diabetic eye examinations
  • Colour Vision assessments
  • Macular Degeneration testsi
  • Glaucoma & eye pressure testing
  • Checks for Cataracts
  • Clearness of vision (myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism)
  • Focusing ability (Presbyopia)
  • Eye muscle movement and control Amblyopia (lazy eye)
  • Contact lens fitting and management
  • Referrals for eye laser surgery
  • Mining & Industrial Safety Spectacles
  • Advice on the correct fitting of good quality sunglasses (both prescription and non-prescription, in both adults and children, particularly in a sporting environment)
